MT651 Sequential Compression Pump

This pump offers to the Medical Health Therapy Sector, an easy to use, in-home relief for venous and lymphatic disorders. Its use is based on a sleeve with three compression chambers inflated from distal to proximal. Its sequential compression increases blood flow and eliminates extra cellular fluid. This 651 Sequential Compression Pump is three times faster than the models currently on the market. The entire inflation/deflation cycle is just 45 seconds. Compression is progressive, with each chamber reaching the same peak pressure. A one year warranty against defective material and workmanship is standard.

Size 11" x  7 3/4" x 3"

Contradictions:
· Severe arteriosclerosis or other  Ischemic Vascular Disease
· Edema due to congestive cardiac failure
· Known or suspected Deep Vein Thrombosis
· Local conditions such as untreated, infected wounds; Gangrene; recent skin graft; dermatitis.
